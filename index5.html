Maya Ali

Ali next again appeared alongside Osman Khalid Butt in the 2013 Haissam Hussain's A-Plus Entertainment serial Aunn Zara (based on a novel Hisar e Mohabbat by Faiza Iftikhar). Ali played the role of Zara, the only daughter of single parent who want to live with a family and thus marries to Aunn and thus leads the couple faces a twist at every turn.
